Design

Hobby

I have a small habit of thinking about things, how they could be used, and what they could be made into. In fact, I’m part of a fandom community that has an interest in anthropomorphic animals, the furry community. And one activity that’s common in the community is fursuiting, an activity that involves wearing an animalistic suit of an original anthropomorphic character. Wearing a full-body suit can get rather warm. And though you can get small DC fans installed into the head to help, ventilation and cooling are an issue with them. So as I thought about it, I thought about the use of compact DC air pumps that can be brought for fish tanks when AC is unavailable. I thought about the use of those styles of pumps being used to force outside air into the suits and, even if it’s not much, improve cooling and ventilation for the suits.

And even if it leads me to ramble on, even when writing (which is a unique talent if I say so myself), it could come to be a useful habit. A habit that has use in everyday life and the workplace.
